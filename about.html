About This Project

This website was created as the Synthesis Project for BIOL 1: General Biology at Butte College. The goal was to curate and create content on a topic at the intersection of biology and society. This project aims to present complex neurobiology in a way that increases both understanding aand empathy.

This site was coded by the author and is hosted on GitHub Pages. All content is based on the research and curation completed during the semester-long project.

About the Author

Author Portrait Placeholder

Nicholas A. Thompson

I am a Cognitive Science student focused on the biological roots of cognitive patterns that are often deeply misunderstood. This project was an opportunity to explore the tangible biology behind what is often a very isolating, internal experience. By building this site, I aimed to practice clear, accessible science communication to help de-stigmatize these biological realities.
